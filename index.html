Tell Me More

Services

Currently we have household goods for incoming families, cooking and English classes, transportation, and upcoming driving training.

Loving

Our mission is to not only help refugees, but to have a lifelong friendship.

Following

In the beginning, we focus on immediate needs; furniture, clothing, cookware and bakeware, etc. However, we know that these are not what refugees truly need. Refugees need to be able to persue a life they had previously been denied.

Sharing

In the end, we hope that the refugees we have helped will take what they have learned and help others.
